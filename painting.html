FAQ

Do you use low-VOC paints?

Yes, premium low-VOC lines with minimal odor and fast curing.

How do you protect furniture and floors?

Plastic wrap, drop cloths, masked edges, and HEPA dust control during sanding.

Will matte show defects?

Matte hides minor texture better than gloss; we still skim and sand for a flawless result.

FAQ

Do you install preserved moss and specialty panels?

Yes. We mount preserved moss and other specialty wallcoverings with proper substrates, edge finishing, and safety clearances.

Will seams be visible in strong daylight?

We plan seam locations away from raking light and use tension control to minimize visibility, especially on plaster-look finishes.

Is grasscloth suitable for bathrooms?

For powder rooms, yes — with proper primer, ventilation, and careful handling. For wet zones we recommend vinyl alternatives with a similar texture.
